# Autohub Features Roadmap

## 🔴 Must-Have (Core MVP)
1. **Multi-user Roles & Permissions:** Authentication and authorization for different roles (e.g., owner, manager, cashier).
2. **Barcode/QR Scanning:** Fast billing and accurate stock counts using device cameras or external scanners.
3. **Purchase Management & Supplier Ledger:** Tracking purchases, supplier balances, and last purchase prices.
4. **Low Stock Reorder Suggestions:** Automated alerts and lists of items that need restocking based on thresholds.
5. **Customer Credit (Udhaar) Tracking:** Managing customer balances with automated SMS/WhatsApp payment reminders.
6. **Reporting:** Day/week/month sales reports with export capabilities (Excel/PDF).

## 🟡 Should-Have (Enhancements)
1. **Mobile App / PWA:** For owners to check live business stats and do quick billing on the go.
2. **Warranty Tracking & Alerts:** Keep track of part warranties and notify customers/staff of expirations.
3. **Basic Analytics:** Dashboards showing top-selling parts, margins per SKU, and growth trends.
4. **Data Import/Migration Service:** Utilities to import CSV/Excel sheets for smooth customer onboarding.

## 🟢 Nice-To-Have (Future Additions)
1. **Loyalty & Discount Programs:** For repeat/regular customers auto-applied during billing.
2. **Multiple Price Lists:** Distinct pricing tiers for retail vs. wholesale customers.

## 📈 KPIs / Acceptance Criteria
- **Retention (Trial → Paid):** ≥ 20%
- **Billing Speed:** Average billing time reduced by 50% vs manual/existing methods.
- **Supplier Order Fulfillment:** Stock fulfillment percentage improves.
